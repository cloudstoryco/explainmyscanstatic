How it works

This page walks you through how to use the service and answers common questions.

How do I use the service?

  1. Sign in using your email (no password needed)
  2. Upload your medical scan (JPEG, PNG, or DICOM)
  3. Our AI analyzes the image and produces:
    • Key findings
    • Diagnostic insights
    • A plain-English explanation
  4. Download or print the report — all within seconds

Do I get free usage?

Yes. Every registered user receives:

  • 1 free scan analysis per month
  • It resets every 1 month from your last upload
  • Free quota is automatically applied — no action needed
  • Unused free reports do not roll over to the next month

What if I want more reports?

You can purchase additional credits from the Payment or Buy Credit page:

  • Choose the number of credits (minimum 5)
  • The system auto-detects your local currency
  • Proceed to secure payment via Stripe
  • Credits are added to your account immediately after successful payment

How are rates calculated?

Credit prices vary slightly depending on your location and currency.
Here’s how it works:

  • The system detects the country from where you access the website from the IP Address
  • Your currency (e.g. MYR, SGD, USD) is auto-selected
  • Rates are based on a predefined cost-per-credit in our database
  • If we don’t have rate for your country, we will default to USD rate

Is my payment secure?

Yes — your payment is 100% secure.

We use Stripe, a world-leading payment platform trusted by millions of businesses including Amazon, Google, and Shopify.

All payments are:

  • 🔒 Encrypted and PCI-compliant (Stripe is certified as a PCI Service Provider Level 1 — the highest level)
  • ✅ Processed on Stripe’s secure infrastructure — your card details never touch our servers
  • 🌐 Handled via HTTPS with end-to-end encryption

We do not store any credit card information on our side. Stripe handles everything securely behind the scenes.

What happens to my data?

We take your privacy seriously.

  • We store zero data by default
    Once your scan is analyzed, neither the uploaded image nor the AI-generated result is stored in our system.
  • No tracking, no profiling
    Your usage is session-based, and we don’t use your scans to train models or build profiles.
  • Enterprise options available
    For clinics, hospitals, or radiology labs, we offer secure integration services that include:
    • Private data storage
    • On-premise deployment
    • Region-specific data residency compliance (e.g., PDPA, GDPR, HIPAA)

✅ Individual users enjoy complete data privacy.
🏥 Enterprise users benefit from custom data storage and security solutions.

For more, visit our Integration page or contact us.

Other questions?

If you have additional questions or need help, please contact us via email. We typically respond within 1–2 business days.
