How It Works

This page walks you through how to use the service and answers common questions.

How do I use the service?

  1. Sign in using your email (no password needed)
  2. Upload your medical scan (JPEG, PNG, or DICOM)
  3. Our AI analyzes and gives:
    • Key findings
    • Diagnostic insights
    • A plain-English explanation
  4. Download or print the report — all within seconds

Do I get free usage?

  • 1 free scan analysis per month
  • It resets based on your last upload date
  • No action needed — quota applied automatically
  • Unused free scans do not roll over

Want more reports?

You can buy credits via the Payment page. Just choose your amount, currency is auto-detected, and pay via Stripe.

Is my payment secure?

  • 🔒 Encrypted and PCI-compliant (Stripe Level 1)
  • ✅ Processed securely via Stripe — no card data stored
  • 🌐 End-to-end HTTPS encryption

What happens to my data?

  • Zero storage by default: scans and results are not saved
  • No tracking or profiling: we don’t use your data for training
  • Enterprise options available for private deployments

✅ Individuals: full privacy
🏥 Clinics: custom storage, region-specific compliance (GDPR, HIPAA)
