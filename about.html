About

Explain My Scan is a privacy-first medical AI assistant. Our mission is to provide instant, secure insights for users who want a second opinion on their medical scans without hassle or delay.

We are a small team of healthcare technologists, developers, and designers who believe that medical information should be easy to understand and accessible — without compromising privacy.
